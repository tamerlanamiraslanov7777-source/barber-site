Our Story

Craft Over
Speed

"We opened Blade & Co. with a single belief: that every man deserves to feel exceptional when he leaves a barber's chair."

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Prague's Old Town, we built our reputation on unhurried appointments, honest conversation, and an obsessive attention to detail. Our barbers train for years before touching a client — and it shows in every line, every fade, every shave.

We use only professional-grade European products. Our chairs are reclined in leather. Your drink is waiting before you sit down.

— Tomáš Novák, Founder
